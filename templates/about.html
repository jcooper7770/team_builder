How it works Leads
These values represent the percentages of meta leads that the given pokemon can beat (in the 1 shield).
For example: "azumarill: 64.512%" means that azumarill beats 64.51% of the reported leads.
Meta Leads
These values represent the number of times each of these pokemon was reported as a lead in gobattlelog.
For example: "stunfisk_galarian: 705.00" means that the galarian version of stunfisk was reported as the lead 705 different times.
